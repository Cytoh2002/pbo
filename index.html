About Us

Global Ascend in Christ is a faith-based Public Benefit Organization based in Kajiado County, Kenya. We are committed to transforming lives through education, community outreach, and Christian leadership.

Legal Compliance

  • Complies with the Public Benefit Organizations Act of Kenya
  • Registered with the Public Benefit Organizations Authority
  • Submits annual reports as required
  • Follows financial and anti-corruption regulations

Our Objectives

Educational Support: School fees, mentorship, and materials for vulnerable children.

Community Outreach: Food programs and medical assistance.

Christian Leadership: Promoting strong moral and spiritual values.

Capacity Building: Empowering communities economically and socially.

Partnerships: Working with government and organizations.
